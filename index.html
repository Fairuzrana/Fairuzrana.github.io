Media Studies

Definition

Media (the singular form of which is medium)[1] is the collective communication outlets or tools that are used to store and deliver information or data to a large number of people. There are basically two types of media- personal and print media.[2][3] It is either associated with communication media, or the specialized mass media communication businesses such as: print media and the press, photography, advertising, cinema, broadcasting (radio and television) and publishing.
